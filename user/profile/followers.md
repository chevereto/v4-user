# Followers

This section displays a list of users who follow you (or the viewed user). Each entry shows the follower's avatar, username, and a link to their profile.

## Accessing Followers

* In the top bar, click on the **User Icon** (requires [Login](../account/login.md))
* Click on **My Profile**
* Click the **Followers** tab on your profile

Or navigate directly to `/{username}/followers`.

## Follower count

The total number of followers is shown on the profile header next to the **Followers** label. This count updates in real time as users follow or unfollow the account.
