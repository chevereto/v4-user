# Notifications

Notifications are located in the top bar, next to the user icon. The icon displays a badge with the count of unread notifications.

<img class="media-screen" src="../../src/manual/settings/user/content/notification-ex.png" width="400"/>

## Accessing notifications

* Click the **bell icon** in the top bar to open the notifications panel (requires [Login](../account/login.md))

## Notification types

Chevereto generates notifications for the following events:

* Someone **likes** your media or album
* Someone **follows** you
* Someone **comments** on your content (when comments are enabled)

## Marking as read

Notifications are marked as read automatically when you open the notifications panel. The badge count resets once all notifications have been viewed.
