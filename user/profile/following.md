# Following

This section displays content uploaded by the users that you follow, giving you a personalised feed of media from your network.

## Accessing Following

* In the top bar, click on the **User Icon** (requires [Login](../account/login.md))
* Click on **My Profile**
* Click the **Following** tab on your profile

Or navigate directly to `/{username}/following`.

## Content

The Following feed shows the latest media from all users you follow, sorted by most recent. When you are not following anyone, or the users you follow have not uploaded content, the listing will appear empty.

## Following a user

To follow a user, visit their profile and click the **Follow** button. See [User Profile](user-profile.md) for details.

## Unfollowing a user

To unfollow a user:

* Visit the profile of the user you want to unfollow
* Click the **Unfollow** button
