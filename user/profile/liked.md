# Liked

This section displays all media and albums that you have marked as liked. It acts as a personal collection of favourited content across the site.

<img class="media-screen" src="../../src/manual/settings/user/content/user-liked.png" width="600"/>

## Accessing Liked

* In the top bar, click on the **User Icon** (requires [Login](../account/login.md))
* Click on **Liked**

Or navigate directly to `/{username}/liked`.

## Liking content

To like a media item or album:

* Click the **heart icon** on any media or album thumbnail
* Or open the media and click the **Like** button, or use the `L` keyboard shortcut

## Unliking content

To remove a like, click the **heart icon** again on any content you have previously liked. The item will be removed from this section.
